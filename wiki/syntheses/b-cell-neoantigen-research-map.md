# B-Cell Neoantigen Research Map

This page organizes the field around research questions rather than disease buckets or journal shelves.

## Core Question

How do we connect neoantigen discovery and therapeutic design with the B-cell and TLS context that may shape whether those interventions work in human cancer?

## Working Model

1. Generate and prioritize the right antigen candidates.
2. Understand the local immune architecture, especially B cells and TLS.
3. Translate targets into interventions such as vaccines or cell therapies.
4. Interpret response through biomarkers, spatial context, and tumor-specific constraints.

## Why This Structure

- Cancer type matters, but it is not the main intellectual scaffold.
- Journals matter for browsing, but they are not the main unit of understanding.
- The main unit should be the research problem and the evidence chain around it.
