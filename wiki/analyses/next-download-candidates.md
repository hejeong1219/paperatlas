# Next Download Candidates

Immediate next-wave download targets drawn from recent high-impact human-cancer literature that fit the current strict filters.

## Key Points

- These candidates are intended to grow the underfilled B-cell neoantigen and ptmanchor anchors first, because resistance already dominates the current ingest baseline.
- All entries below were selected to stay inside the current exclusion rules as far as the presently visible metadata allows.
- This list is a working acquisition queue, not a final endorsement of every paper's eventual centrality.

## B-Cell Neoantigen Proposal Candidates

- [Atezolizumab plus personalized neoantigen vaccination in urothelial cancer: a phase 1 trial](https://www.nature.com/articles/s43018-025-00966-7)
  - Why it matters: human cancer phase 1 trial linking personalized neoantigen vaccination to a clinically relevant immunotherapy setting.
  - Anchor: [B-Cell Neoantigen Proposal Anchor](./b-cell-neoantigen-proposal-anchor.md)
- [Mature tertiary lymphoid structures evoke intra-tumoral T and B cell responses via progenitor exhausted CD4+ T cells in head and neck cancer](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-025-59341-w)
  - Why it matters: recent human tumor TLS paper that strengthens the B-cell and TLS side of the proposal.
  - Anchor: [B-Cell Neoantigen Proposal Anchor](./b-cell-neoantigen-proposal-anchor.md)
- [Proteogenomic analysis reveals RNA as a source for tumor-agnostic neoantigen identification](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-023-39570-7)
  - Why it matters: human pan-tumor proteogenomic neoantigen study that directly strengthens the proposal's RNA-plus-proteogenomics logic.
  - Anchor: [B-Cell Neoantigen Proposal Anchor](./b-cell-neoantigen-proposal-anchor.md)
- [Proteogenomic discovery of neoantigens facilitates personalized multi-antigen targeted T cell immunotherapy for brain tumors](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-021-26936-y)
  - Why it matters: human tumor proteogenomic neoantigen pipeline paper with translational cell-therapy linkage, useful for the proposal's antigen-discovery side.
  - Anchor: [B-Cell Neoantigen Proposal Anchor](./b-cell-neoantigen-proposal-anchor.md)

## ptmanchor Manuscript Candidates

- [Comprehensive evaluation of phosphoproteomic-based kinase activity inference](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-025-59779-y)
  - Why it matters: directly relevant to protein-aware phosphosite interpretation and kinase inference across matched cancer proteomic and phosphoproteomic cohorts.
  - Anchor: [ptmanchor Manuscript Anchor](./ptmanchor-manuscript-anchor.md)
- [Deciphering the dark cancer phosphoproteome using machine-learned co-regulation of phosphosites](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-025-57993-2)
  - Why it matters: recent cancer phosphoproteome paper that should inform PTM-aware signal interpretation beyond simple abundance shifts.
  - Anchor: [ptmanchor Manuscript Anchor](./ptmanchor-manuscript-anchor.md)

## Triage Notes

- The two B-cell candidates are immediately useful because the dedicated B-cell neoantigen cluster is still very small.
- The two ptmanchor candidates are immediately useful because the dedicated PTM-oriented cluster is still effectively empty under the current baseline.
- Resistance remains important, but the next marginal value is higher in the underfilled anchors.
