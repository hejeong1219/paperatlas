# Anchor Keyword Clusters

Working keyword clusters for the three active seed projects so new papers can be acquired and linked in a more consistent way.

## Key Points

- The resistance manuscript should pull from human cancer studies where resistance mechanisms converge on immune evasion, antigen presentation failure, immune exclusion, immune-cold genotypes, and biomarker fragmentation.
- The B-cell neoantigen proposal should pull from human cancer studies linking neoantigen discovery, proteogenomics, B-cell or TLS biology, spatial or single-cell immune context, and translational vaccine or T-cell-readout work.
- The ptmanchor manuscript should pull from human cancer studies focused on PTM-aware cancer proteomics, phosphoproteomics, protein-normalized signaling interpretation, kinase inference, and pan-cancer cohort analysis.
- These clusters are meant to guide acquisition, not to replace deeper source-page judgment.

## Exclusion Rules

- Exclude mouse or murine tumor models.
- Exclude xenograft or PDX studies.
- Exclude organoid studies.
- Exclude other preclinical-only systems when there is no durable human cancer evidence core.
- Exclude low-priority venues such as MDPI journals, PLOS ONE, and Scientific Reports.
