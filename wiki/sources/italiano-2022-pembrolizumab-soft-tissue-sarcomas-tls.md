# Pembrolizumab in soft-tissue sarcomas with tertiary lymphoid structures: a phase 2 PEMBROSARC trial cohort

Italiano et al. is one of the clearest clinical demonstrations that TLS are not just correlative pathology features but can enrich a trial cohort for checkpoint benefit.

## Key Points

- This phase 2 PEMBROSARC cohort enrolled patients with advanced soft-tissue sarcoma selected for intratumoral `TLS` presence.
- The 6-month non-progression rate was `40%` and the objective response rate was `30%`, meeting the primary endpoint.
- These outcomes were far better than the previously reported all-comer PEMBROSARC cohorts, where both non-progression and response rates were very low.
- Exploratory analyses further suggested that intratumoral plasma-cell abundance tracked with better outcome inside this TLS-selected population.
- The paper is important because it converts TLS from a descriptive biomarker into a concrete patient-selection strategy.
