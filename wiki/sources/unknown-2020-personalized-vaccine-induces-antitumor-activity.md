# Personalized Vaccine Induces Antitumor Activity.

_Cancer discovery, 2020._ PMID: [32576589](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/32576589/).

DOI: [10.1158/2159-8290.CD-NB2020-064](https://doi.org/10.1158/2159-8290.CD-NB2020-064)

## Summary

A personalized cancer vaccine, when combined with the PD-L1 inhibitor atezolizumab, has shown early promise in patients with solid cancers: In a phase Ib trial, the therapy induced neoantigen-specific T-cell responses-and elicited complete or partial tumor eradication in some patients.
