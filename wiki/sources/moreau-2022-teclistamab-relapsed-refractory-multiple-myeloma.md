# Teclistamab in Relapsed or Refractory Multiple Myeloma

This pivotal MajesTEC-1 report positions BCMA-CD3 bispecific redirection as a scalable alternative to BCMA CAR-T in heavily pretreated myeloma, while also clarifying its infection and cytopenia liabilities.

## Key Points

- Enrolled 165 patients with relapsed or refractory myeloma after at least three prior therapy lines and widespread triple-class exposure.
- Produced an overall response rate of 63.0%, with 39.4% achieving complete response or better.
- Reached MRD negativity in 26.7% of all treated patients and in 46% of those with complete response or better.
- Median duration of response was 18.4 months and median progression-free survival was 11.3 months in a heavily pretreated population.
- Toxicity was dominated by cytokine release syndrome, cytopenias, and infections rather than high-grade neurotoxicity, helping define the operational tradeoffs of off-the-shelf T-cell redirection.
