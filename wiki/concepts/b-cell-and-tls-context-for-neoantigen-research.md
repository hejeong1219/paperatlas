# B-Cell and TLS Context for Neoantigen Research

This axis asks how B cells, plasma cells, TLS maturity, and spatial immune organization shape the success or failure of neoantigen-directed immunity.

## Key Questions

- When do B cells and TLS improve antitumor immunity?
- Which TLS features matter most for response?
- Can strong neoantigen candidates fail because the local immune architecture is weak?
