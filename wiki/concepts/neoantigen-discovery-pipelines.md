# Neoantigen Discovery Pipelines

Neoantigen discovery pipelines convert tumor sequencing and peptide-presentation evidence into prioritized therapeutic targets for vaccines, T cell therapies, and translational immunology studies.

## Key Points

- Strong pipelines integrate genomics, transcriptomics, HLA context, and ideally immunopeptidomics.
- Candidate generation should include canonical and noncanonical peptide sources when feasible.
- Prioritization is distinct from generation; both ranking quality and experimental validation matter.
- Shared neoantigens and private neoantigens support different therapeutic strategies.
- Clinical usefulness depends on both upstream antigen selection and downstream immune context.
